About the Project

Smart Water Quality Monitoring System

This CBTP (Community-Based Technology Project) aims to develop a real-time water quality monitoring system using IoT technology. The system continuously tracks various water parameters and provides instant alerts when quality degrades, contributing to water conservation and public health.

CBTP Project Objectives

Water Quality Assessment

Develop a system to monitor key water quality parameters in real-time

IoT Implementation

Apply IoT concepts and embedded systems for environmental monitoring

Data Analysis

Collect and analyze water quality data for community awareness

Community Impact

Contribute to public health through water quality monitoring
